Know your Salmon

There are five species of Pacific Salmon:

  • The mighty King or chinook-salmon
  • Red or Sockeye-salmon
  • Silver or Coho-salmon
  • Pink or Humpback-salmon
  • Chum or Dog-salmon (Not available at Pat's!)
